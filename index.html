About Us

We believe that health is the key. And thats why we work to provide top-notch and economical pharmaceutical products. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful idea: Great companies start because founders want to change the world, not to make fast buck.

Our roots and growth

With the goal to serve people around the globe Kish Pharma was formed in 2023 by four family members. We believe that everyone should have access to quality medicines whether it be for managing disease, fighting infections, or simply improving overall health.
